Thomson Reuters· CoCounsel, UAE market translate →

"CoCounsel is impressive, but it can't walk us through anything UAE-specific. Why demo it here at all?"

Not a product flaw, but a localization gap. Selling readiness the product didn't have yet would cost more than naming the gap honestly. Reset the enablement narrative for the region and fed the local-content gap back to product as a roadmap input, instead of overselling in the room.

· Account-based marketing translate →

"It's the same five accounts showing up at every regional event, in every market."

The highest-intent segment wasn't the widest one. It was the most repeated one. Reallocated ABM spend toward that segment; it went on to drive 30% of annual MENA pipeline.

· Retail, Djibouti translate →

"Every competitor on the shelf makes the same three claims: growth, digestion, immunity."

DHA was the one differentiator competitive analysis found that none of them were messaging. Translated into product messaging and sales enablement materials built for the local market.

Renault Digital· EMEA performance translate →

"Conversion drops at the same step, in every country dashboard, every month."

A UX friction point disguised as a market-by-market performance problem. Ran it as an A/B test with CRO instead of a local campaign fix. The pattern was the signal.
